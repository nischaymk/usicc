Internship at CEAS

Gain valuable experience and mentorship at the Center for East Asian Studies

About the CEAS Internship Programme

The CEAS Internship Programme is highly competitive, particularly beneficial to students and younger researchers from diverse backgrounds. Interns will get an opportunity to develop various skill sets during the programme. They will be mentored by senior scholars and paired with other interns for collaborative research. A certificate will be provided on satisfactory completion of the internship.

"Center for East Asian Studies provided me with constant guidance and support throughout my internship, and the members of this Center are rooted in the vision of delivering impactful innovations and solutions to global challenges in East Asia. Proudly be a part of this team."

Roles and Responsibilities

Interns at CEAS will have the opportunity to contribute in various capacities, including:

Research and Publications

  • Conceptualizing research ideas
  • Conducting academic research
  • Writing and publishing research papers, perspectives, and working papers

Social Media & Website Management

  • Updating and managing the CEAS website
  • Creating and curating content for social media outreach

Administration

  • Mentoring new interns and team members
  • Tracking and managing ongoing activities

Events

  • Organizing academic events
  • Planning and coordinating logistics
  • Executing event-related tasks efficiently

Logistics Support

  • Assisting in operational and logistical requirements for events and projects

Projects

  • Writing proposals and reports
  • Executing and managing project deliverables

Internship Details

Eligibility Criteria

Open to students pursuing Undergraduate, Master's, or PhD programs. Selection is purely merit-based. Candidates with a strong spirit to learn and grow will be given high priority.

Duration

The internship is flexible, with a minimum commitment of one month.

How to Apply

Send an email expressing interest to contact.ceas@christuniversity.in with the required documents. Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview.

Required Documents
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Original writing sample (~2000 words)
  • Statement of Purpose
